Why Choose a Home EV Charging Station?

Faster Charging

Charge your electric vehicle from 0% to 100% in as little as 6 hours, compared to over 24 hours with standard plug sockets.

Cost-Effective

Save money with overnight charging at lower electricity rates, reducing your annual EV energy costs to as little as £200-£300.

Convenient & Smart

Enjoy advanced features like remote control via apps, scheduled charging, and real-time usage stats.

Available Models

Our EV charging stations are designed to meet your needs. Choose from a range of reliable and efficient products:

  • Premium Rapid Charger: £2,000 - Ultra-fast charging speeds with added smart features.
